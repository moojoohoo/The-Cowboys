Round One

Cowboys Panthers
0 24

The Cowboys played awful. They made 18 errors, missed 42 tackles, and conceded 7 penalties, which are all leading the league. The Panthers, on the other hand, made 17 errors, missed 16 tackles, and conceded 3 penalties. Kurt Capewell scored a quick try, but the rest of the first half was tryless. The Panthers were up 8-0 at halftime. Then, Dylan Edwards, Brian To'o, and Isaah Yeo all went over in a ten minute period to win the game for the Panthers. The Panthers won 24-0.

Round Two

Cowboys Dragons
18 25

Both teams played okay. Jason Taumalolo's injury really hurt the Cowboys. Coen Hess went over in the second minute off of a beautiful kick from Jake Clifford. Mikaele Ravalawa went over in the tenth minute and Josh Kerr in the twenty-seventh minute for the Dragons. Corey Norman kicked a field goal on the half-time buzzer. Dragons were up on the break, 13-6. Valentine Holmes then scored seven minutes after half-time off of a little grubber he put in himself. Ben Hunt went in on the fifty-third minute to keep the Dragons at a comfartable lead. The Cowboys quickly struck back with Jake Granville going in just four minutes after Ben Hunt. Zac Lomax then went over in the sixtieth minute to keep the Dragons at an comfartable lead. Dragons then defended for next twenty minutes to win, 25-18.

Round Six

Cowboys Bulldogs
30 18

The Cowboys have got back-to-back wins!! Kyle Feldt scored the first try off of a beautiful kick from Ben Hampton in the sixth minute. Renouf Atoni went over in the twelve minute for the 'Dogs. Both teams gritted out the rest of the first half. The halftime score was 6-6. Four minutes after the break, Reece Robson threw a beautiful dummy and scored a great try out of dummy-half. Jake Granville made a beautiful play that sent a charging Ben Condon over in the fifty-second minute. Six minutes later, the Cowboys played a nice play. Jake Granville had passed out of dummy-half to Scott Drinkwater, who passed the ball on the inside to Valentine Holmes who made a great break and Jake Granville supported the play. Valentine Holmes put a beautiful pass to Jake Granville, ran thirty metres to score a beautiful try. Kyle Flanagan and Tuipulotu Katoa went over in the 63' and 69' minute. Mitch Dunn scored in the seventy-eighth minute to give the Cowboys the win. The Cowboys won 30-18!
