Round Three

Posted on 29th of May
Cowboys Titans
36 6

The cowboys played pretty good. They scored quite a few good tries and had pretty solid defense. Reece Robson played a great game scoring two trys. The titans didn't do the best in defense or offense.

Round Four

Posted on 6th of June
Cowboys Sharks
16 26

The cowboys didn't play great. We needed to defend Jesse Ramien and Ronaldo Muitlino better. We had some good trys like Kyle Feldt's 65 metre try and Justin O'neill's good kick chase but not enough for the cowboys. The sharks had pretty solid defense and attacking offense.

Round Five

Posted on 12th of June
Cowboys Warriors
26 37

The cowboys did okay. They scored some great trys like Valentine Holmes' nice catch and Hamiso Taubai-Fidow's quick hands to Justin O'Neill to score. The warriors' Peta Hiko scored a hat-trick which supported the warriors win.

Round Six

Posted on 28th of June.
Cowboys Tigers
20 36

The Tigers where unstoppable in the first half scoring six trys. The Cowboys Didn't do very well due the absense of Valentine Holmes and Michael Morgan. The Cowboys inproved in the second half scoring four trys but that wasn't enough.

Round Seven

Posted on 28th of June.
Cowboys Knights
32 20

The Cowboys played excellent in the first half scoring 26 points. Scott Drinkwater, Jake Clifford, and Hamiso Taubai-fidow were on fire with try assists and trys. The Knights fought back but Coen Hess scored in the second half which gave the Cowboys the win.

Round Eight

Posted on 3rd of July.
Cowboys Eels
4 42

The Cowboys didn't play well with bad defense and little errors. The Eels played very well with great defense and unpredictable attack. Maika Sivo played very well scoring four trys.

Round Nine

Posted on 9th of July.
Cowboys Roosters
16 42

The Roosters played really well with Matt Ikuvalu scoring an amazing five trys which four of them happened in second half. The Cowboys played very gritty but Reece Robson played okay scoring a double.

Round Ten

Posted on 19th of July.
Cowboys Panthers
10 22

The Panthers were good as always. Jarome Luai scored in the first half, and Brent Naden, Stephen Crichton and Nathan Cleary all scoring in the second half. The Cowboys played well in the first half with Hamiso Tabuai-Fidow scoring a beautiful try. The Cowboys went into half 4-4 and Daejarn Asi scored quickly after halftime but the Cowboys weren't able to hold on to the Panthers strong attack.

Round Eleven

Posted on 24th of July.
Cowboys Sea Eagles
12 24

The Sea Eagles started off strong with Curtis Sireon and Danny Levi Fast. Kyle Feldt scored right before halftime. The Sea Eagles went into half time leading 12-6. DCE and Cade Cust scored next a little after halftime. Coen Hess dropped the ball over the line which wasn't helpful. Francis Molo charged over the line, and Cowboys tried hard to fight back, but weren't able to pull it off.

Round Twelve

Posted on 1st of August.
Cowboys Raiders
12 14

The Cowboys kept trying to pull it of put couldn't do so.
